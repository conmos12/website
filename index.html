Hello, I am Connor Mose. I was born in Auckland and moved to Whangarei when I was 13. I attended Robertson Road School for my primary school years [years 0- 6]. Kedgley Intermediate for Years 8 & 9 then attended Aorere College for half a year before moving to Tauraroa Area School where I finished my schooling career.




I currently live in Maungakaramea Whangarei about 2 and a half hours from auckland. This is a wonderful little country settlement 20 mins south of Whangarei. We have roughly around 400 people in the little town. The local primary school is Maungakaramea and the College is Tauraroa Area School which I attended.
